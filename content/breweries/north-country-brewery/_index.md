Nestled in the heart of downtown Slippery Rock, North Country Brewery typically features 10-15 beers that rotate seasonally.  While this unique restaurant and brewery inhabits a former funeral home, it is quite lively inside.  Featuring a great menu and fun events, North Country Brewery is a great stop for any beer lover.
